Weather factors and air pollutants by day

In the first graph of this section, we represent the daily average values of one weather variable and one air pollutant.
The values are represented as parallel coordinates, as an additional tool to try to understand the degree of correlation between a meteorological variable and air pollution.

The aim of this section is to visualize and understand the conditions of different days.
In second graph of this section, we get an overview of all the meteorological conditions, in the days we are examining

How to use : At the start, all days are displayed, cluttering the graph. You can pick a starting and ending date, to examine a limited number of days.
You can also choose the best or worst N days to be displayed.
