Crime in San Fransisco

San Fransisco is a city in the West Coast of the United States. It is the financial, cultural and commercial centre of Northern California. It is the 13th most populous city in the US. Due to its closeness to Silicon Valley, it has experienced an influx of people over the years. Thinking about moving or visiting San Franscisco on your next vacation, check its crime staistics below.


In this article, we have created visualizations on information of crime committed in the San Fransisco area. The information used in the visualizations are gotten from sf_crime.geojson, that contains 10000 randomly sampled,geocoded crimes in San Fransisco. Also, we have a visualization gotten from sfpd_districts.geojson that contains the police districts that make up the city.


To see the first visualization scroll down or click on the bouncing arrow below.

Crimes Committed in San Fransisco :


This visualization shows the crimes committed in the San Fransisco area within a period of time. These 10000 different crimes were committed during a period of time, on separate days and different locations around the city. The visualization shows them all at the same time. This visualization also contains interactive elements such that hovering over a point gives the user the basic information such as the address of where the crime was committed, the description of the crime and the police district the area falls under. From this visualization, we can find out the crimes committed based on the days of the week, the category of the crime and the police district where it falls. There are also histograms of these selection criteria which gives the user the required information in summary.

Police Districts:


This visualization shows the land area of the 10 police districts in San Fransisco.


The police districts are each in the shape of polygons. Hovering over each polygon reveals the name of the corresponding police district. From the visualization, we are able to discover that the smallest police district based on land size is Tenderloin.
